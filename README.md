# pust

A minimal breathing app featuring three different patterns to help you find your calm.

**[Try the app live here](https://pust-app.netlify.app/)**

## Breathing Patterns

- **4–4**: A light and steady pace for a 5-minute session. Ideal when you want a simple rhythm to settle into.
- **5–5**: A balanced 5-second inhale and 5-second exhale pattern. This 5-minute session is designed for finding a steady, calm flow.
- **4–7–8**: A specific technique featuring a 4-second inhale, 7-second hold, and 8-second exhale. This 4-cycle session is designed to help you wind down and relax.

## Features

- **Session Selection**: Choose between three different breathing rhythms directly from the menu.
- **Light & Dark Mode**: Automatically adapts to your system’s color scheme.
- **Screen Wake Lock**: Prevents your screen from dimming or turning off during your session.
- **Zero Dependencies**: Built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

## Getting Started

Simply open `index.html` in any modern web browser to choose a pattern and start your session.
