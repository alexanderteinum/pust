# pust

A minimalist breathing app designed to help you find your calm.

**[Try the app live here](https://pust-app.netlify.app/)**

## How it works

- **Pure Focus**: No settings or instructions. Just a simple 4–4 breathing rhythm.
- **Start**: Tap “Start” to begin your session (38 full breath cycles).
- **End**: Tap the circle anytime to return to the start screen.

## Features

- **Light & Dark Mode**: Automatically adapts to your system’s color scheme.
- **Screen Wake Lock**: Prevents your screen from dimming or turning off during your session.
- **Zero Dependencies**: Built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
